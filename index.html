Who We Are

Your One-Stop Electrical & Lighting Store

At BABJI TRADERS, we are the proud wholesalers and retailers of electricals and lighting materials in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh. Whether you're building a new house, renovating your home, or setting up a commercial space — we have everything you need.

From basic wiring accessories to the latest LED fixtures, fancy chandeliers, switches, MCBs, and fans — our shelves are stocked with every kind of electrical and lighting product used in constructing and illuminating a house.
